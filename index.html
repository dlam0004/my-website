Features

Chat

Stay connected with friends through seamless text, voice, and video calls. Engage in dynamic, customizable group chats that put you in control of your social interactions.

Posts

Stay connected with real-time social interactions and discover, gatherings and activities tailored to your interests. Organized by people who share your interests. LIFEHUB is your space to make things happen—meet like-minded people, create your own groups, and bring your community together. Control who sees your posts and who can join your activities. It's not about big events; it’s about connecting in meaningful, everyday ways that enrich your life.

Private Tab

Unlock your potential with personalized journal prompts, designed to inspire reflection and growth. Capture and cherish your most meaningful memories as you gatherthem, while curating and customizinf vision boards to manifest your dreams.

Personal Management

Take control of your day with a fully customizable calendar, complete with reminders and a well structured priority list. Streamline your daily tasks, enhance productivity, and improve the execution of your goals.

Notifications

Stay in the loop with instant notifications! Whether it's a new connection request, an exciting event update, or a helpful calendar reminder, you'll never miss a beat on LIFEHUB.

Power Up Your Profile

Username

Using real names helps create a safe, more connected community. Let others know exactly who they are interacting with, building trust and authentic relationships. Let us work on building an open but safe community where genuine relationships can grow.

Location

Completing your city location helps ensure you receive relevant content and connections in your area. While your exact address remains private, sharing your city allows you to discover local events and meetups. Adding details like your school or workplace, with the option to keep them hidden, will enhance personalized recommendations based on your interests

Exercise Control

As the post author, you decide who can join and participate. Customize your privacy and control over interactions to create the experience you want.
